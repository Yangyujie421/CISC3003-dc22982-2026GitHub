Applying tips

Apply through the official company careers page whenever possible, not third-party aggregators. Referrals dramatically increase your probability of a first-round interview — ask classmates and alumni in your network.

Most new-grad programmes open applications in July/August for the following summer's start date. Assume every deadline is earlier than advertised; internal quotas fill fast.
